How it works

When you visit a website that has audio (music, podcast, Noheh), the extension detects it and asks for permission to cache. Once granted, every audio file you visit on that site is automatically downloaded to ~/Downloads/CHYMUSIC/audio/ (or to your chosen folder if the standalone PWA is open and connected).
